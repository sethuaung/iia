A Risk-Focused Approach

Our annual audit plan is built on a rigorous risk assessment that prioritizes the most critical areas for our business. This ensures we focus our resources where they can add the most value and provide the greatest protection against potential threats.

The radar chart illustrates the key areas of focus identified in our FY 2026 risk assessment, with a higher score indicating greater inherent risk and audit priority.

The Four-Phase Audit Lifecycle

Every audit engagement follows a structured, four-phase methodology. This ensures consistency, quality, and a clear line of communication from initial planning through to the verification of corrective actions.

1

Planning

Defining audit scope and objectives based on the risk-based Annual Audit Plan.

2

Fieldwork

Executing tests using standardized checklists to gather evidence and identify findings.

3

Reporting

Communicating findings and securing management's commitment to corrective action plans.

4

Follow-Up

Tracking all action plans in the Follow-Up Log and verifying their effective implementation.

Building a Culture of Compliance

A strong governance framework is supported by a culture where every employee is empowered to uphold our standards. Our Code of Ethics, mandatory training, and protected reporting channels are the cornerstones of this culture.

Confidential Reporting Channels

Our Whistleblower Policy guarantees non-retaliation for any employee who, in good faith, reports a concern through one of our secure channels.
