The Annual Internal Audit Plan

Our annual audit plan is developed through a risk-based approach, focusing resources on areas with the greatest potential impact on our strategic goals. This ensures we address the most significant risks facing our distribution of Warrix and the growth of our Rhino, Fit, and Goal brands.

The Core Audit Process

Each audit follows a structured, five-phase process to ensure consistency, thoroughness, and transparency from start to finish.

1

Planning

Define scope, objectives, and criteria. Conduct preliminary risk assessment.

→

2

Fieldwork

Gather evidence through testing, interviews, and observation.

→

3

Analysis

Evaluate evidence, identify root causes, and assess business impact.

→

4

Reporting

Communicate findings and recommendations to management.

→

5

Follow-Up

Track implementation of agreed actions to ensure risks are mitigated.
